Hi!
I'm <b>Talha</b>.
I'm a Control engineer by training, and I enjoy studying mathematical intricacies and expressing them through the medium of programming.

Brief Background
================
I'm a PhD student in Computer Science and Fulbright Scholar at [**Purdue University**](https://www.purdue.edu), where I am advised by [**Dr. Ahmed H. Qureshi**](https://qureshiahmed.github.io).

Previously, I was a Machine Learning Software Engineer at [**Pointivo**](https://pointivo.com/) focusing on developing and deploying 2D/3D objection localization and scene segmentation pipelines.
I did my Bachelors in Mechatronics & Control Engineering from the [**University of Engineering and Technology, Lahore**](https://www.uet.edu.pk), and my Masters in Electrical Engineering from [**Syed Babar Ali School of Science & Engineering, LUMS**](https://sbasse.lums.edu.pk).
